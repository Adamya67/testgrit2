About the CIT

Explore the Center for Information Technology’s purpose, curriculum, and community at Deep Run High School.

Our Mission

The Center for Information Technology (CIT) at Deep Run High School in Glen Allen, Virginia, is a highly selective specialty center for students passionate about technology. CIT develops advanced technical and soft skills to prepare students for success in college and professional careers. Core values like respect and integrity drive its curriculum and culture.

Curriculum

The CIT curriculum combines academic rigor with real-world relevance, offering IT and computer science courses that challenge students to push their limits. CIT alumni consistently share how the program’s coursework and experiences prepared them for higher education and industry careers.

Community & Experience

The CIT extends learning beyond the classroom with Community Innovation Technology projects and Senior Internships. Students collaborate with real clients, apply agile methodologies like Scrum, and gain hands-on experience solving community challenges. The tight-knit CIT community also enjoys events like the Thanksgiving potluck and field trips, balancing hard work with camaraderie.
